Parking in the Airport

Parking Services at Prishtina International Airport

Whether you are traveling, or you are meeting or greeting your loved ones at the airport, Prishtina International Airport provides the opportunity for a short stop and longer stay of your vehicle in a parking space with 1,200 marked parking lots and 6 marked lots for buses.

Payment procedure for the parking services is simple and easy with the automated parking payment machines that you can find in front of, as well as in the terminal building.

To use the parking services, in advance reservation is not required.

Parking

The parking is located right in front of the terminal building, just across the transit street and the greenery. Here you can park your vehicle for a shorter period, 1 hour, 2 hours or more during a day, but also, depending on your needs, you can park your vehicle for a longer time.
