Covid-19 Rules

Before you travel

  • Get a negative test up to 72 hours before your flight
  • If you are getting symptoms related to Covid-19, please reconsider if you want to treavel
  • Make sure your airline company are commited to practising health measures that reduce the spread of Covid-19

During your flight

  • Frequently sanitize your hands. Use a hand sanitizwers that is at least 60% alcohol.
  • Wear a mask at all times. Know how to correctly remove your mask after each use.
  • Avoid contact with people that are not respecting the rules. Also try to keep a physical distance of 2 meters with others.
